# Soul of Universe - LMS Platform Design Documentation

## Project Vision
A modern, minimalist Learning Management System (LMS) designed for high-impact mentor-student interactions. The platform prioritizes ease of use, clear scheduling, and high-quality session playback.

## Brand Identity: "Soul of Universe"
- **Voice & Tone:** Professional, calming, and intellectually stimulating.
- **Brand Name:** Soul of Universe
- **Product Type:** Dual-interface LMS (Admin & Student)

## Design System
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Soft Blue (#2196F3) for actions and navigation.
  - Surface: Pure White (#FFFFFF) and very light gray (#F7F9FB) for content cards.
  - Background: Minimalist light background to reduce cognitive load.
  - Text: High-contrast dark gray for readability (#191C1E).
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Manrope/Inter) for a clean, tech-forward feel.
- **Component Style:**
  - Rounded corners (large radius) for a soft, approachable look.
  - Flat design with subtle tonal shifts instead of heavy shadows.
  - Glassmorphism effects (backdrop blur) on top navigation bars.

## Screen Breakdown

### 1. Student Dashboard (MVP)
The core hub for students, focusing on immediate next steps.
- **Knowledge Feed:** Displays the next two high-priority sessions ("Class with Jendh" and "Call with Sharan") with prominent "Join" buttons.
- **Booking Grid:** A real-time availability calendar for scheduling new sessions with mentors.
- **Quick Assistance:** Integrated chat support for student help.

### 2. Admin Master Scheduler
The command center for mentors and administrators.
- **Weekly Availability:** A drag-and-drop calendar interface for setting time blocks.
- **Quick Schedule:** One-click functionality to broadcast new slots or notifications to students.
- **Request Management:** Sidebar for tracking incoming session requests from students.

### 3. Video Library (Recorded Sessions)
A "Netflix-style" repository for asynchronous learning.
- **Featured Workshop:** High-impact hero section for the most recent or important recording.
- **Categorization:** Sessions organized by "Recent" and "Series Spotlight".
- **Search & Discovery:** Easy-to-use search filters for finding specific topics or mentors.

## Interactive Prototype Features
- **Cross-Platform Navigation:** Seamless switching between the Dashboard, Library, and Scheduler.
- **Actionable UI:** Integrated "Join" and "Book" flows for a functional user experience simulation.
